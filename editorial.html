Editorial

Seema Asthana

Editor

Dear Friends in Rotary,

It is with great pleasure that I present before you the District Directory for the Rotary Year 2017-18 with the hope that it will prove itself a complete vessel of all information that Rotarians will need for successful carriage of their rotarian duties and obligation. We have tried our best to update all the information.

District Directory can not be written overnight. The wheels start rolling months in advance. It requires meticulous planning and execution. We had a tough time in collecting the data. Generally, we found that our Rotarians don't give adequate importance and priority in providing the correct information on time. But as the saying going ‘All is well, that ends well’. We were able to collect as much data as we could.

Although we have taken utmost care to provide accurate details and present before you an errorless document. But inspite of our efforts, being human, we are prone to commit mistakes. Hope you will overlook them as ‘To err is human, to forgive divine’.

At last I thank God for giving me wisdom to become a part of such a diverse & versatile community of Rotarians dedicated to the service of mankind. Wishing you a very Happy and Purposeful Rotary year 2017-18.

ROTARY CAUSES
