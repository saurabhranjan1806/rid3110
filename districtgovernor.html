DISTRICT GOVERNOR

Vinay Asthana

DISTRICT GOVERNOR 2017-18

MPHF Rtn. Vinay Kumar Asthana is the Charter President of Rotary Club of Kanpur Shikhar. He was born in Meerut in a very reputed Kayastha Family of Shree Surendra Nath Asthana on 10th September 1961. He is the youngest among three brothers and sisters. He had his basic education in Lakhimpur Kheri and graduation from Christ Church College, Kanpur. He completed his professional education in Prosthetic & Orthotic Engineering from All India Institute of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation, Mumbai in the year 1985. He started his professional carrier as a Rehabilitation Professional and established a center in the name of Shree Viklang Kendra at Kanpur.
Rtn. Asthana joined Rotary in the year 1991 as a member of R.C. Kanpur Central and was its member till 2004 -05. In the year of 1994 -95 he took the charge of Honorary Secretary of the club. In the year of 1997 he became the President of the Club.
After successful Presidential tenure Rtn Asthana proved his metal beyond club level and became GGR (Governors Group Representative) in the year 1998. And there was no looking back. In the year 1999 he was appointed District Secretary. In the year 2001 he was nominated Assistant Governor. In the year 2002 he was District RYLA Chairman and member nominating committee for DGN. Rtn Asthana was nominated District Secretary in the year 2004 , Assistant Governor in the year 2012 -13, District Secretary as well as Zonal Chairman Intercity in the year of 2013 -14.
In the year 2009 Rtn Vinay Asthana was instrumental in starting Rotary Club of Kanpur Shikhar and became the Charter President. In his dedicated Rotary life of more than two decades he led the clubs four times as President, he has been District Secretary thrice, Assistant Governor twice and member of different committee many times. In the year 2000-01, Rtn. P. V. Purushottam, Chairman National Polio Plus Committee appointed Rtn. Asthana as his representative to visit Mau Nath Bhanjan where he convinced the minority community members to give Polio drops to their children. For this he was given a letter of Appreciation.
Rtn. Vinay Asthana is member of many civil and professional organizations. He is Life member of OPSI (Orthotic & Prosthetic Society of India), OPAI (Orthotic Prosthetic Association of India), RCI (Rehabilitation Council of India) and member of ISPO (International Society of Prosthetic & Orthotic). He is the first professional from India to be appointed as Advisor to the “Business World P & O” a monthly journal magazine published from USA. Rtn. Vinay Asthana is also a Life member of Kayastha Mahasabha, he also served Bharat Vikas Parishad Panchyjan Branch as Secretary.
In the year 1990 Rtn Asthana was felicitated and awarded by the Governor of U.P. His Excellency Sri Satya Narayan Reddy, for the Herculean work done by him in the field of Rehabilitation of Physically Challenged people. Rtn. Asthana is married to Rtn. Seema who is also from a family of Rotarians and is a past president of R.C. Kanpur Shikhar. They are blessed with a brilliant son Karan and a lovely daughter Vasundhara.
